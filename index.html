Give back and support those who have supported you.

Arizona has the worse students to counselors ratio in the nation. School counselors do a lot for students. Kids4Counselors aims to give students the opportunity to give back to their school counselors. With over 300 student members across the state, Kids4Counselors is one of the fastest growing student staff support organizations in the state of Arizona. Kids4Counselors has several member districts across the state.
